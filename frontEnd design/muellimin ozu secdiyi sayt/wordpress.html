WordPress

Wordpress is among the most prominent open source content management systems all around the globe and it is tremendously used for simple websites, blogs, complex portals and enterprise websites, and even applications. Wordpress comes up with easy to use architecture, many amazing features, templates and customization.
WordPress is a fully customizable content management system and offers a unique platform that can be easily altered for any need and requirement.Wordpress has extensive plugin directory, a powerful template framework with an easy to get administrative dashboard. WordPress powers more than 30% of the web — a figure that rises every day. Wordpress also maintains a worldwide community to help you 24*7. Numerous plugin are available to add-on features to Wordpress panel.
